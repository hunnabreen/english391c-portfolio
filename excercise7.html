Global Economic Data Research Project
In this project write-up for my Discrete Mathematics course, we had to propose a project that would incorporate Discrete Mathematics as a part of the study. My partner and I decided to run some studies about global economic data, using the the World Development Indicators data set from the World Bank. How we carried out the project and the results of our research are found in this write-up.


Coral Reef Research Paper

In this paper, I did some extensive research on what the main causes of this steep decline of coral in the oceans, no surpise that it is almost all brought on about because of human activity.
