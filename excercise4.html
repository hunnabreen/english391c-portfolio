Campus Record Label


Concrete Valley Records is UMass' student run record label. The website would boast colors that resemble UMass, however it be embellished with its own unique creative side. The record label would have liken itself to any other student run organization, however it would have to display somewhat of a mission statement, and a roster of artists on the label. These artists would attend the five college consortioum, and there would be a page that had a list on the label. There would be an about page, that had all of the mission statements of the label, and would have profiles on all of the faculty of the label. There would also be a contact page, where people can send demo tapes of their music, as well as general content.
